About The Project...

What is the Project..?

This project involves the preparation of Bakelite, a synthetic polymer, through a chemical reaction between phenol and formaldehyde. The experiment demonstrates the principles of polymerization and the properties of thermosetting plastics.

Why this Project..?

This project is significant because Bakelite was the first commercially produced synthetic polymer, revolutionizing the plastics industry. By preparing Bakelite, we can understand the chemical processes involved in its production, its properties, and its applications. This project also helps develop laboratory skills, such as measuring, mixing, and heating chemicals.
